About Us

who we are ?

● The beginnings - the story of the Wild Circus :

  • Wild Circus was born at Paris, France in 1978. For 40 years the company has been putting up extraordinary shows and successfully touring them both nationally and internationally.From New York to South American rainforests, Madrid to outback France, Wild Circus has taken its self-crafted performances of wit, grace and spectacle to over 27 countries across five continents, to critical acclaim.
  • From New York to South American rainforests, Madrid to outback France, Wild CThe Wild Circus show is a rock-n-roll, all human circus, presenting shows for people of all ages.Celebrating breathtaking stunts, irreverent humour, cracking live music and an all human ensemble, Wild Circus promotes the best of the French spirit: generosity, diversity, death-defying bravery, and a fair go for all.
  • Wild Circus has a strong belief in tolerance, diversity and human kindness. For many years the company has engaged in issues associated with social justice and a good time for all, including work each year with many charities, indigenous communities and the raising of over $500,000 in donations to support refugees and asylum seekers.
